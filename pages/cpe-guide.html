SC-CPE — CPE Submission Guide

Use the steps below to submit your CPE/CEU credits to your certification body. Fields are pre-filled from your certificate — just copy and paste.

Period—
CPE hours—
Sessions—

Submit to CompTIA CE Portal

  1. Go to ce.comptia.org and sign in
  2. Click Submit a CE Activity
  3. Fill in the fields below (click to copy):
Activity Title
Provider
CEU Amount
Date Range
Verification URL
  1. Upload your signed PDF certificate as evidence: Download PDF
  2. Click Submit

Submit to ISC2 CPE Portal

  1. Go to cpe.isc2.org and sign in
  2. Click Report CPE Credits → Education
  3. Fill in the fields below (click to copy):
Activity Name
CPE Credits (Group B)
Completion Date
Verification URL
  1. Upload your signed PDF certificate: Download PDF
  2. Click Submit

Submit to ISACA CPE Portal

  1. Go to isaca.org/credentialing/cpe and sign in
  2. Click Report CPE Hours
  3. Fill in the fields below (click to copy):
Activity Title
Activity Type
Provider
CPE Hours
Completion Date
Description
Verification URL
  1. Upload your signed PDF certificate: Download PDF
  2. Click Submit

Acceptance is ultimately the certification body's decision — see Terms §5.
